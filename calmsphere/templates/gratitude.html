Gratitude Meditation

5–10 minutes  ·  No props needed

Gratitude shifts the lens through which you see your life. This practice invites you to pause, reflect, and let the warmth of thankfulness fill your heart. What you appreciate, appreciates.

1 Arrive & Breathe

Sit comfortably and close your eyes. Take three slow, deep breaths. Place one hand on your heart. Feel the warmth of your palm and the natural rhythm of your heartbeat.

2 Three Gratitudes

Bring to mind three specific things you are grateful for. They can be small (a warm cup of tea) or large (a loved one's smile). For each one, pause and truly feel the gratitude in your body — a softening, a warmth, a lightness.

3 Rest in Gratitude

Let go of the specific things and simply rest in the feeling of gratitude itself. Let it radiate from your heart through your whole being. Stay here for a few minutes, breathing gently, smiling softly.

Gratitude turns what you have into enough. Feel it fully.

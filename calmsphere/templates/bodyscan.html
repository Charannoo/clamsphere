Body Scan Meditation

15–20 minutes  ·  Best done lying down

The body scan is a gentle journey through your own body, inviting you to notice sensations without trying to change them. Tension lives where attention has not gone. Let awareness be your healing touch.

1 Feet & Legs

Lie down on your back, arms at your sides, eyes closed. Bring your awareness to your toes. Notice warmth, coolness, tingling, or nothing at all. Slowly move up through the soles, ankles, calves, knees, and thighs — spending 2–3 breaths on each area.

2 Torso & Back

Shift attention to your hips and lower back. Feel the rise and fall of your belly with each breath. Scan up through the abdomen, chest, upper back, and shoulders. Notice any tightness and simply breathe into it.

3 Arms, Neck & Crown

Move through your fingers, hands, wrists, forearms, elbows, and upper arms. Then bring awareness to the neck, throat, jaw, and face. Finally, rest at the crown of your head. Feel your body as a whole — alive, breathing, at ease.

Wherever you are in your body, be there fully. That is enough.

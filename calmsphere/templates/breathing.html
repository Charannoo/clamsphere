Breathing Exercises

5–10 minutes  ·  Simple, powerful techniques

Your breath is your anchor — always with you, always available. These two powerful techniques can calm your nervous system in minutes. Practice them anytime you need to reset.

① 4-7-8 Breathing — The Relaxing Breath

Sit comfortably with your back straight. Exhale completely. Inhale quietly through your nose for 4 seconds. Hold your breath for 7 seconds. Exhale completely through your mouth for 8 seconds with a whoosh sound. Repeat for 4-5 rounds. This technique activates your parasympathetic nervous system.

② Box Breathing — The Navy SEAL Reset

Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds. Hold your breath for 4 seconds. Exhale through your mouth for 4 seconds. Hold your lungs empty for 4 seconds. Visualize tracing a box with your breath — each side is one count. Repeat for 5-10 rounds. Excellent for focus and anxiety.

The breath is the bridge between body and mind. Cross it anytime.
